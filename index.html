Speakers

Oren Rubin

Oren Rubin

Oren has over 16 years of experience in the software industry, started with Distributed Systems, Compilers, Image Processing, he has been focusing on Web Development in the last 6 years. Although being a busy entrepreneur, Oren always finds time to help the community: he manages both the Israeli Selenium and Ember meetups, frequently lectures in other meetups too, and teaches Angular.js at the Technion.

Shai Reznik

Shai Reznik

Angular.js Consultant for enterprise companies, Shai have more than 10 years of experience writing code. After 7 years of being the co-founder & CTO of 2 (fat) startups, Shai discovered the Lean Startup methodology and fell in love. Currently working on his latest business - HiRez.io, which is an online architecture resource site for large scale companies.

In the past year & a half Shai lectured about Angular, Javascript and Entrepreneurship in various meetup groups and universities, and lead 2 meetup & facebook groups (Javascript Israel, Talking Lean).

In his free (free?) time, Shai performs improvisation shows at the Improv House (improvhouse.co.il), which he co-founded.

Miško Hevery

Miško Hevery

Miško is the creator of AngularJS framework. He has passion for making complex things simple. He currently works at Google, but has previously worked at Adobe, Sun Microsystems, Intel, and Xerox, where he became an expert in building web applications in web related technologies such as Java, JavaScript, Flex and ActionScript.

Ran Mizrahi

Ran Mizrahi

Passionate entrepreneur and software engineer who loves to continuously innovate and deliver meaningful products while having true fun with the right team.

Ran embraced AngularJS about two years ago and since then gave multiple lectures and delivered successful web project built on top of AngularJS.

Over the years Ran led, architected and hands-on developed multiple successful products that were delivered to the hands of millions (such as the Wix App Market and much more).

Asaf David

Asaf David

Asaf is an entrepreneur and a full stack developer. Mamram graduate, over 10 years of experience, founder and CTO of TixWise and Bink. Web consultant.

Lukas Ruebbelke

Lukas Ruebbelke

Lukas Ruebbelke is a technology enthusiast and co-author of AngularJS in Action for Manning Publications. His favorite thing to do is get people as excited about new technology as he is. He runs the Arizona Software Community User Group and has hosted multiple hackathons with his fellow partners in crime.

Uri Shaked

Uri Shaked

Uri loves the web. Started developing software at the age of 12, he has since been pursuing his passion for technology, both professionally and through notable side projects and community activities. He is a lean startup enthusiast, the lead of Tel Aviv’s Google Developer Group and is an Angular.JS expert who lectures in an advanced web development code lab at the Technion, Israel’s Institute of Technology.

Among his interests are reverse engineering, hardware hacking, and mobile development. Uri is also an avid Salsa dancer and a musician; he often likes to bring his passions together in projects such as the SalsaBeatMachine.org.

Alon Heller

Alon Heller

Fullstacker with emphasis for web UI

Worked in small startup companies as well as big enterprises and gained great experience doing what he loves - building applications.

During the last 6 years he developed web applications optimized for virtualization environments and hybrid cloud solutions (VMware & Alcatel-Lucent).

In my spare time he likes to: Ride his Bike, Play Squash, Losing in a PES game to his son.

Nir Kaufman

Nir Kaufman

JavaScript Developer & AngularJS evangelist.

Nir started as a flex developer, back in the days that Flash ruled the web front-end.

in the past two years Nir Lives & breathes Angular 25/7 as a developer, consultant and instructor. In his spare time he likes to explore new ideas and technologies, play guitar and cook vegan food.

Roy Peled

Roy Peled

Roy works with Angular.JS for a over than 2 years.

He designed and implemented 2 major platforms using Angular.JS, the first for Conduit, and he currently works on a second one at Folloze.

He moderates a AngularJS Facebook group with over 7,000 users.

ng-coding Workshop

Hack on Angular.JS. Help solving issues and adding features. Get prizes.

ng-coding is a live coding session is all about giving back to the community. We will work on Angular.JS and related open-source project, fix bugs, add features and create new directives, tools and modules for the community.

Workshop participants will enjoy one-on-one mentoring from industry experts, top Angular.JS hackers and other talents. It is your opportunity to learn from the best, get some hands-on experience with building open-source projects, and perhaps even win some prizes.

We will give away a Nexus 7 and Angular.JS branded SWAGs to the top performing participants.
